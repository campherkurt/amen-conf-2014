    Conference

    The AMEN Bible Conference.

    It seems like yesterday when we had the 2013 AMEN Bible Conference. Indeed, the quick passing of time is a clear indicator that our Savior is even at the door.

    The bells of the Conference doors are ringing again, but better still are the bells towards the doors to eternity. AMEN is endeavoring for attendees at this year’s conference to not only learn to have the mind of Christ, but also help others know how to think with an eternal perspective.

    Why should you attend?

    • To be revived! Four international Speakers, some of our best local speakers and a team of volunteers prepared to bless us with a super-charged spiritual experience.
    • To learn! 6 training sessions to choose from, including Bible prophecy, preaching, music ministry, health outreach and personal evangelism.
    • To meet a friend in Jesus! Join us and mingle with other like-minded Christian friends who all desire to spread the gospel message and see the soon return of our Saviour.

       

    • To reach a soul for Jesus! This year the AMEN Conference will be taking Cape Town by storm.

      Each morning a team will hit the trains and buses of Cape Town spreading the good news about Jesus through sharing and literature, AND on Monday, 15 December 2014, we will be embarking on our very first Mission Monday.

      On Mission Monday the conference will be moving into the Cape Town CBD in a massive outreach effort to touch soul for the kingdom through an explosion of health expo's, music ministry, literature distribution and personal witnessing.

    Speakers

    Learn about the speakers at this year's conference.

    Wes Peppers

    Wes Peppers

    "Ps Wes Peppers currently serves as a pastor in the Michigan Conference and formerly as the Global AFCOE Coordinator at Amazing Facts. He became a Seventh-Day Adventist Christian at the age of 22 after being an atheist/agnostic for several years.

    Soon after accepting Christ, quitting college football and a life of reckless living, he entered into full time ministry. He has served globally as an evangelist, speaker, and AFCOE teacher.

    A recent cancer survivor at age 28, he has a powerful testimony of God's goodness and a renewed passion for His message. His greatest desire is to lead young people into a deeper, intimate relationship with Jesus Christ and teach them how to share Him with others.

    Ps Wes Peppers will be presenting a breakout on Last Day Events and will be presenting during the plenary sessions."

    Marion Peppers

    Marion Peppers

    "Marion is a concert vocalist who has chosen to use music to glorify God and bring people closer to Jesus. She has sung around the world for ministries including It Is Written, Amazing Facts, 3ABN television, and numerous radio stations across the U.S.

    She has also done Bible Work, colporteuring, and evangelism training. Her husband, Wes, is a pastor in the Michigan Conference where they currently reside. They have recently had the joy of having their first son, Levi.

    Marion's greatest desire is to share the Gospel of Jesus Christ through song and hopes that each will leave her concerts loving Jesus more. Marion will be presenting breakout session on music."

    Alan and Nicole Parker

    Alan and Nicole Parker

    Alan and Nicole Parker live in Tennessee, where Dr. Parker is a professor in the School of Religion at Southern Adventist University. He is also the Director of the Pierson Institute of Evangelism and Missions as well as Director of the Soul-winning and Leadership Training (SALT) Program at Southern.

    Nicole recently finished her master’s degree in Biblical counseling and is currently working on a second MA in Pastoral Ministry. Her testimony of healing from abuse has inspired others to find healing in God’s love. She is a stay-at-home mother of their three children.

    They have a passion for reaching out to young people through discipleship. They enjoy reading, hiking, playing with their children and sharing their love of Jesus with others.
