    Conference

    The AMEN Bible Conference.

    The AMEN Bible Conference 2013 is built on a theme, which expresses the very heart, mind and thought of God towards mankind. God is an evangelist at heart in the sense that He longs for each person to be saved and experience salvation through Christ.

    With this being the core yearning of God's heart, AMEN is endeavoring for attendees at this year’s conference to experience the practicality and relevance of the gospel through the heart of God, EVANGELISM.

    Evangelism is more than just an occasional witness through preaching, handing out tracts, singing etc. it is a result of a life that displays a growing relationship with Jesus, and through a relationship with Jesus your life becomes a witness to the world of the love of God and the salvational power of God through Jesus Christ.
    For the experience of radical discipleship, a deeper sense of devotional life and a greater love for God through the empowering of the Holy Spirit, AMEN Bible Conference 2013 is this beginning.

    Let us relive Pentecost! Let us experience the Great Commission! Let God’s Commission be Our Missions! Let it be so! Let it be AMEN (Advent Message to Every Nation)!
