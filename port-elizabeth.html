    Conference

    The AMEN Bible Conference.

    AMEN (Advent Message to Every Nation) is a ministry that aims to inspire and equip young people to spread the gospel in their own homes, churches, communities, schools and workplaces.

    There are various activities that facilitate this throughout the year in various churches such as weeks of prayer, evangelism workshops, health training, etc.

    All these activities create a build-up for the conference which happens annually during December in Cape Town. At the AMEN conference, young people from all over the country have the opportunity to come together to be inspired and trained to spread the gospel by experienced speakers and teachers from afar and those who are local.

    The Lord has lead AMEN to an exciting new venture: spreading her wings to Port Elizabeth for the first time. We will have a conference there from 5 – 7 December and the one in Cape Town will run from 12 – 15 December.

    Come join us in the Friendly City, a coastal beauty in the Eastern Cape, to be trained and inspired to spread the Good News to every nation, kindred, people and tongue – an instruction given by God to each of us.

    Speakers

    Learn about the speakers at this year's conference.

    Wes Peppers

    Wes Peppers

    "Ps Wes Peppers currently serves as a pastor in the Michigan Conference and formerly as the Global AFCOE Coordinator at Amazing Facts.

    He became a Seventh-Day Adventist Christian at the age of 22 after being an atheist/agnostic for several years. Soon after accepting Christ, quitting college football and a life of reckless living, he entered into full time ministry.

    He has served globally as an evangelist, speaker, and AFCOE teacher. A recent cancer survivor at age 28, he has a powerful testimony of God's goodness and a renewed passion for His message.

    His greatest desire is to lead young people into a deeper, intimate relationship with Jesus Christ and teach them how to share Him with others. Ps Wes Peppers will be presenting a breakout on Last Day Events and will be presenting during the plenary sessions."

    Marion Peppers

    Marion Peppers

    "Marion is a concert vocalist who has chosen to use music to glorify God and bring people closer to Jesus.

    She has sung around the world for ministries including It Is Written, Amazing Facts, 3ABN television, and numerous radio stations across the U.S.

    She has also done Bible Work, colporteuring, and evangelism training. Her husband, Wes, is a pastor in the Michigan Conference where they currently reside. They have recently had the joy of having their first son, Levi.

    Marion's greatest desire is to share the Gospel of Jesus Christ through song and hopes that each will leave her concerts loving Jesus more. Marion will be presenting breakout session on music."

    Ryan McCarthy

    Ryan McCarthy

    "My name is Ryan McCarthy from Port Elizabeth. I'm studying Nursing Science so that I may be a medical missionary. I believe we are living in the last days of earth's history and soon the health ministry will be one of only two ways left to reach people for Christ."

    Jonathan & Tarryn Brydon

    Jonathan & Tarryn Brydon

    "Jonathan & Tarryn live in Port Elizabeth and are members of the Kabega Park SDA Church.

    Tarryn is from Cape Town and Jonathan is from the UK. They met in Scotland, and when Tarryn had to return to her studies in Cape Town in 2006, Jonathan decided to move to Cape Town.

    They have been married for five years and are blessed by their one year old daughter Hannah. Tarryn is currently the children's ministries coordinator at Kabega, while Jonathan serves as an elder and in church communications.

    Having had recent experience of the challenges that face young people, Tarryn and Jonathan see youth ministries as an area of much importance in the church today."
